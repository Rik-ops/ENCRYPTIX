About Narendra Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi, born on 17 September 1950, is India's 14th and current Prime Minister. His journey from a humble tea seller to the nation's top leader reflects his determination, strategic thinking, and unwavering dedication to national service.

As a reformer and a strong proponent of digital innovation, his policies have transformed India's global image and improved millions of lives.
